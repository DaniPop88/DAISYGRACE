Refund & Return Policy

We want you to shop with confidence. If there’s ever an issue with your order, we're here to make it right.

DAISYGRACE ESSENTIALS STORE values your satisfaction. If you’re not completely happy with your purchase, please review our refund and return guidelines below to understand what you can expect.

Eligibility for Refunds & Returns

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of receipt.
  • Products must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.
  • Refunds are available for products that arrive damaged, defective, or incorrect.
  • Personal care items that have been opened cannot be returned due to hygiene reasons.

How to Request a Refund or Return

  1. Contact our support team at contact@daisygracestore.com within 7 days of receiving your order.
  2. Provide your order number, photos of the product (if applicable), and a brief explanation of the issue.
  3. We will review your request and guide you through the next steps.

Processing Refunds

Once your return is approved, we’ll send you instructions for shipping the item back to us. After we receive and inspect the product, eligible refunds will be processed within 7 business days. Refunds are credited to your original payment method.

Exceptions

  • Sale or promotional items are non-refundable unless damaged or incorrect.
  • Opened or used personal care products cannot be returned.

Questions or Support

If you have questions about our refund policy or need help with a return, please reach out. Our team is ready to assist you.
